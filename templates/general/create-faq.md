Create a FAQ with {{number_of_questions}} questions and answers about {{topic}} aimed at {{audience}}. The questions should be ones that someone unfamiliar with the topic would genuinely ask. Answers should be clear, direct, and avoid unnecessary technical language. Order them from most basic to most specific.
