Create a comprehensive checklist so {{responsible_role}} can successfully complete {{task_or_process}}. Context: {{context}}. Group items into logical phases or categories. Each item should be actionable and verifiable. Include steps that are often forgotten or overlooked. The goal is for someone to follow it without needing additional instructions.
