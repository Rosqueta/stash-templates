---EN---
Prepare the agenda and materials for the kickoff meeting of {{project_name}}, with the {{involved_team}} team, with a delivery date of {{delivery_date}}. The meeting should cover: context and why this project matters, clear objectives and success criteria, scope and what is out of scope, team roles and responsibilities, key milestones and timeline, identified initial risks, and ways of working and communication channels. Include questions to open discussion in each section.
