---EN---
Facilitate a retrospective for {{team_name}} covering the {{period_reviewed}} period using the {{preferred_format}} format. Generate the questions or activities for each phase: what went well, what did not work, what we can improve, and action commitments. For each identified improvement area, help formulate a concrete action item with an owner and date. The goal is for the team to leave with 2-3 actionable and achievable commitments.
