Organiza los hallazgos en: patrones de comportamiento principales, necesidades y motivaciones identificadas, pain points y frustraciones, y oportunidades de diseño detectadas. Synthesize the findings from a {{research_type}} study conducted with {{number_of_participants}} participants about {{research_topic}}. Organize findings into: main behavior patterns, identified needs and motivations, pain points and frustrations, and detected design opportunities. For each finding indicate the supporting evidence and confidence level. End with the 3 most important implications for design or product.
